New Delhi, NCT Delhi

New Delhi is the capital of India, located in the National Capital Territory of Delhi. There are many different sites to see in New Delhi. As a lot of governmental sites are there, there are some monuments that are specifically focus on a larger number of Indians rather than a specific historical figure. For example, the India Gate is a monument that was built as a way of remembering the 80,000 soldiers that were killed during the World War I.

The Qutab Minar is a monument that was built by Qutub-ud-Din Aibak after the Muslims defeated the last Hindu leader in Delhi. The monument is 73 meters tall, which makes it the tallest minaret in the world.

New Delhi is also home to one of the most important temples for Hindus, known as the Akshardham Temple. Akshardham means the divine home of God, and the temple is a place of worship and a spiritually enriching experience for Hindus and non-Hindus alike. Alongside the temple and different educational exhibitions, the temple also holds a fun water show after Sunset, which tells a story through lights, video projections, sounds, and water jets.
